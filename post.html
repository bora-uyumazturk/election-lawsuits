Tracking the Trump campaign's post-election lawsuits

Since the November 3rd General Election, President Donald Trump's legal team and various GOP groups have filed dozens of lawsuits challenging the results input in key swing states such as Pennsylvania, Georgia, and Arizona. These lawsuits generally challenge the validity of the votes cast, seeking to overturn enough votes to put the results in doubt. Using data from democracydocket.com, we looked at the progression of the legal strategy over time looking for trends and patterns.
